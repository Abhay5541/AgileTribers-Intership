About SkillForage

SkillForage is a pioneering job simulation platform dedicated to preparing the workforce of tomorrow. Founded in 2020, we've helped over 50,000 users gain practical experience and confidence in their chosen fields.

Our mission is to democratize access to career preparation by providing affordable, high-quality simulations that replicate real job experiences. We partner with industry leaders to ensure our content remains relevant and up-to-date with current workplace demands.

What sets us apart is our focus on experiential learning. Unlike traditional training programs, SkillForage puts you in the driver's seat, allowing you to make decisions, solve problems, and see the consequences of your actions in a risk-free environment.
